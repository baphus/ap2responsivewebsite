About Me

Hello! You can call me Sephus. I'm the person behind this blog, where I share my passion for music, coding, movies, and other interests that make life enjoyable.

By day, I study as an Information Systems undergrad in Cebu Technological University. In my free time, I enjoy playing my electric guitar, watch movies that incite the inner cinephile in me, and continuously learning programming languages and techniques.

This blog started as a way to document my journey and share what I've learned along the way. Whether you're a fellow enthusiast or just curious about these topics, I hope you find something interesting or useful here.
